About IETE


The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognised professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication & IT. Founded in 1953. The IETE is the National Apex Professional body of Electronics and Telecommunication, Computer Science and IT Professionals. It serves more than 1,25,000 members (including Corporate, Student and ISF members) through various 63 Centres, spread all over India and abroad. The Institution provides leadership in Scientific and Technical areas of direct importance to the national development and economy. Government of India has recognised IETE as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and also notified as an educational Institution of national eminence.

OBJECTIVES:

The objectives of IETE focus on advancing electro-technology. The IETE conducts and sponsors technical meetings, conferences, symposia, and exhibitions all over India, publishes technical journals and provides continuing education as well as career advancement opportunities to its members.

VISION:

Its vision is to reach the unreached and empower the youth through Technical Education and Skill development. MISSION:

MISSION:

Mission for Advancement of Electronics, Telecommunication, Information Technology & other related disciplines; to contribute in Nation's Human Resource & Infrastructure Development through our Engineers & Scientists.

About SST


Space science and Technology (SST) is Space technology wing acts as a platform for the future engineers in providing a strong fundamental system encouraging collaborative research and development programs to innovate and integrate. Imparting an interactive learning system- advice assistance and references for their research development. we organize different orientation courses, workshops, talk sessions with professionals and other making a student to explore themselves.

OBJECTIVES:

We aim to promote the pursuit of excellence and innovation through a unique learning environment. The prime objective of the wing is to create awareness about it and it's applications- allowing them to discover new opportunities.

VISION:

The wing envisions - to advance the knowledge and educate students for the standards and evaluate an integrated system as a part of value added education ensures to their success/overall development.

MISSION:

In this space technology wing contributes to promote space science, astromony and related technologies, by establishing different outreach programs empowering researchers.
